Famous Food of Punjab

Sarson da Saag & Makki di Roti

A traditional winter dish, Sarson da Saag is made from mustard greens and served with Makki di Roti (cornbread). It's topped with butter or ghee and enjoyed with jaggery and buttermilk.

Chole Bhature

A favorite Punjabi breakfast dish, Chole Bhature combines spicy chickpeas (chole) with deep-fried bread (bhature). It's filling, delicious, and packed with flavor.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a rich and creamy tomato-based curry with tender chicken pieces. It's one of Punjab's most iconic and internationally loved dishes.

About Punjab

Punjab, the land of five rivers, is a vibrant blend of traditions, history, and hospitality. Known for its iconic Golden Temple, it symbolizes peace and spirituality.
